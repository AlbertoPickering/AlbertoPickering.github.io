Alberto: I called the "Interlock Solar Track = IST" years ago. While I was playing with My Children We put a pocket Kodak solar charger on top of the toy train, at some moment the charger ended in the floor and the idea became a design instantly in My mind. Is as simple as this; Use all the existing possible railways to install linear solar farms, interconnected solar panels in between the tracks. I started sketching in Photoshop using My Wacom Cintiq 12WX and one of My iMacs. The simple lines described My original idea of a curved solar panel (an arched surface with the lower top close to the inner faces of the train track rails and the panel apex in the middle) the panel will not interfere with the function of the train (of course all the train cars should be inspected and pass security high check points before riding on top of the rails with IST) each panel is interconnected to the next panel, internal wiring and tubing carries electricity, current, ground, data fiber optic lines, water and IST system monitoring sensors lines. The double or triple redundant lines (according to the location requirements like weather) will keep the system functional. The panels are covered by layers of a compound modelled polyacrylic that resists impact from debris, rocks, etc., Temperature changes create difference in the IST gaps and the panels connectors are made from a flexible high density rubber that adapts and absorbs vibrations, as well as the base of the modules avoiding harmonics in the steel rail tracks. The IST also measures the rails distance and creates a 3D reference of the whole line by measuring every meter of it making the train track safer than ever, predicting any eventuality. It works as a weather station too thanks to thermometers and light sensors. The IST panels along the long train tracks receive an enormous amount of solar energy that creates a high power grid; if the power grid is carrying more energy than usual, before getting to risk levels it can be regulated by IST stations that receive IST sensors and communicate satellites, fiber optic data lines hubs and hi voltage power stations that can regulate by storing energy in charging farms or sending power to traditional power grids the water lines can serve linear farming along the train tracks (safe high impact temperature resistant tubes carrying enough water to serve IST stations and have a line of vegetation parallel to the tracks at a distance that will not obstruct the function of the trains or the exposure of the panels to the sky) The IST Stations connect towns and cities. IST uses the same land. IST linear exposure creates more energy because of its extension compared to a concentrated area solar farm. IST works cooler than traditional solar farms. IST can be applied to highways after regulations and safety laws respect the system. On highways it can power electric cars directly (if electric vehicles include a ISTHC "Interlock Solar Track Highway Connector" like toy electric race tracks) or indirectly by charging vehicles in IST stations or changing vehicles batteries if electric vehicles standardize universal power. Of course IST on trains can create electrical training too. I made sketches and a few 3D models years ago, about a decade, and I shared them in my website, social media and with Family and Politicians and companies in different continents. I saw about 2 years ago that in Switzerland something similar was new. IST is one of My projects in standby. What do You thing about it?
